Junhao Chen



I am a junior undergraduate at Tsinghua University. I have had the privilege of being mentored by Professors Zhiyuan Liu and Yulia Tsvetkov throughout my undergraduate years. My research interests lie mainly in the fields of NLP, with a particular focus on long-context models, interpretability, image generation and RL.

Beyond NLP, I am also deeply intrigued by VLMs, especially in the context of efficient architectures for image generation, as well as VLA research--where embodied AI meets reinforcement learning for real-world reasoning and interaction.

Outside of academics, I enjoy playing basketball, jogging, and engaging in board games.
